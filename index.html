About Our School

Alymbek datka lyceum, is one of the leading educational institutions focused on modern science education.

Chemistry plays a key role in our academic program, helping students develop analytical thinking, creativity, and scientific curiosity.

Chemistry Classes

Our chemistry lessons combine theory with practice. Students perform real experiments, work in laboratories, and explore chemical reactions in a safe environment.

Topics include organic chemistry, periodic table, reactions, and modern scientific applications.
